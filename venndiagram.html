APA Format (7th Edition)

  • Best for: Psychology, Education, Sciences
  • In-text: (Smith, 2023, p. 45)
  • Focus: Recency of research, empirical studies
  • Page numbers: Required for direct quotes
  • Title page: Always required
  • Headings: 5 levels of formatting
  • Numbers: Spell out below 10
  • Bias-free language: Emphasized throughout

MLA Format (9th Edition)

  • Best for: Literature, Humanities, Arts
  • In-text: (Smith 45)
  • Focus: Author authority, textual analysis
  • Page numbers: Always included in citations
  • Title page: Usually not required
  • Containers: Flexible source organization
  • Numbers: Spell out if can be written in 1-2 words
  • Inclusive language: Promotes respectful terminology
